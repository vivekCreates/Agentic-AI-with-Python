Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that focuses on creating machines and software capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include learning from data, recognizing patterns, understanding natural language, making decisions, and solving problems. AI systems use techniques such as machine learning, deep learning, and neural networks to improve their performance over time without being explicitly programmed for every situation. Today, AI is widely used in various fields like healthcare, education, finance, and transportation—for example, virtual assistants, recommendation systems, self-driving cars, and medical diagnosis tools. While AI offers many benefits such as increased efficiency and automation, it also raises concerns about privacy, job displacement, and ethical use. Overall, AI is transforming the way humans interact with technology and shaping the future of the world.
